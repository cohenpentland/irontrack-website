Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I track a workout?
A: Tap "Start Workout" on the home screen, then add exercises and log your sets.

Q: Can I create custom exercises?
A: Yes! Go to Exercises → Create Exercise, or create one during a workout.

Q: How do I schedule routines?
A: Open a routine, tap Edit, and select the days you want to schedule it for.

Q: What's included in Premium?
A: Unlimited routines, unlimited habits, and in-depth workout statistics.
