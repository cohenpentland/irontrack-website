Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

IRON TRACK does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All workout data, routines, exercises, and habits are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

2. In-App Purchases

IRON TRACK uses Apple's StoreKit 2 for in-app purchases. All payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not use cloud storage, analytics services, or third-party data collection tools.

4. No Tracking

IRON TRACK does not use tracking technologies, cookies, or analytics services. We do not track your usage or behavior.

5. Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time through the Settings screen. Uninstalling the app will also remove all local data.

6. Children's Privacy

IRON TRACK is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: support@irontrack.app
